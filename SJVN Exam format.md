

##### **CBT GD & PI**
In the SJVN Executive Trainee selection process, the weightage of different stages is as follows:

- Computer Based Test (CBT) score holds 75% weightage.
- Group Discussion (GD) carries 10% weightage.
- Personal Interview (PI) carries 15% weightage.
    
##### **Technical vs SJVN and GK**

For SJVN Executive IT interview and GD preparation, a balanced approach is best: approximately 60% focus on technical (Computer Engineering) topics and 40% on SJVN-related general knowledge and current affairs.

Technical Preparation (60%)
- Prepare well on core Computer Engineering subjects relevant to your degree and projects, such as ==programming==, ==data structures==, ==networking==, ==databases==, ==OS==, and cybersecurity.
- Be ready to discuss your B.Tech projects, internships, and any technical skills in detail.
- Expect scenario-based technical questions to test problem-solving and applied knowledge.

This ratio helps you demonstrate strong technical competence while showing well-informed interest in the company and sector, which the interviewers and GD assess critically. Tailor the mix as you feel more confident in any section but maintain this balanced coverage for the best impression.
